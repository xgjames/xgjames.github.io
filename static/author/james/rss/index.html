I just got the notion of adding one more post here.

  1. Private journal. Like the third tip in How to Dissuade Yourself from Becoming a Blogger, I do have a journal for life and work. There have been a lot of txt files naming by date since 2012, and it keeps growing. Still sometimes it feels right to post something longer than a tweet on the Internet.

  2. Time and efficiency. I sincerely agree with the third and fourth reason in 10 Reasons Not to Write A Blog. Efficiency is crucial for getting self-organized and finding more time. Focus is important, also.

  3. Target market / readership. Things are getting complicated here. There are way too many topics I find myself interested in, yet too few of which I am really proficient in. I started self-studying to become an iOS developer in October, 2013. I got the full-time job last June, which I am still working on now. It is kinda fancy and trendy to run a tech blog as a developer, yet I find myself hesitating. I have no idea who I am writing to or I should write to. Hope I could find out sooner.

Anyway, I will keep this blog updated as I could. Good night.
